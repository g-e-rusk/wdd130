About Me

Geri Rusk

Hi! My name is Geri and I am from California (born and mostly raised here). I am pursuing my BS in Applied Technology. I dabbled in html in the early 2000s when I worked for a commerical computer repair company. In late 2003 I became a stay-at-home mom when my second child was born. I went back to school and in 2007, I received my BA in English with the intention to get my teaching credentials. Four children later, and homeschooling all of them have taught me that I did not want to get into teaching. I have always been interested in Web Design; I am leaning towards that aspect of the Applied Technology field.

I am happily married to my best friend and we just celebrated 25 years this past June. With 1 kid already out of the house, and two more leaving this next year, I am working on increasing my skills and becoming more marketable in the job field. When I am not studying, or homeschooling, I like to read, watch TV, listen to music, exercise, and I am trying to return to digital scrapbooking. Last year I went to my first concert ever, and since then I have been to 4 total, with 3 more planned concerts. I love to laugh and tell jokes. I have been a member of the church my entire life. While in my senior year of high school, I came across one of my favorite scriptures at Seminary. It is Mosiah 4:27 - "And see that all these things are done in wisdom and order; for it is not requisite that a man should run faster than he has strength." It was one of the first times that the scriptures really spoke to me. I have had more experiences in the scriptures since then. I am looking forward to learning as much as I can.
